Act 01 Insertion T-04:12:00

At 19:14 local, Marcus Vale walked through the south service entrance of the Hôtel Hermitage. The cover held. The floor manager glanced at the Lumière credentials, asked a single question about the firm's recent placements — a Marini bronze, quietly handled, Antwerp — and waved him through.

The team filtered in across the next eighty minutes, each operative arriving by a different ingress your dossier had mapped. The service elevator on the east face. The kitchen access via the staff yard. The discreet stair from the lower bar that staff used between courses.

None of you came through the front. As planned.

Act 02 First Contact T-02:43:00

By 20:30, Vale was in the upper salon. The host — known to your team from the dossier as Antoine Renoux — recognized him from a Geneva auction your research had flagged in week three. Two short sentences of mutual recall and Vale was inside the room's confidence.

The art-finance probe Cipher warned about did surface. A Belgian collector asked Vale's opinion on the recent Marini sale at Sotheby's Zurich. Vale's prepared response held — just — though Renoux watched the exchange closely afterward and did not look away for some time. The collector's name had not appeared in your bidder dossier.

Cipher transmitted, low channel, into Vale's ear: "Watch the Belgian. He wasn't on the list."
